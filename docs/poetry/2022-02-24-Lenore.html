Lenore

Posted on February 24, 2022

I dare say I am in a spot of bother,
for I know your fate goes yonder,
Yet everyday my love grows fonder,
Oh! So ever fonder, for my sweet Lenore.

And I might not know where,
But I have no shred of doubt,
That wherever you may fare,
You’ll remain without looking
any worse for wear.

Still, my heart aches as I picture
you on some faraway land,
sipping on some local herb mixture
or digging your feet in darkened sand.

How I wish I could be by your side!
But I have long faded away, since
the day death was easier than life,
and I commited suicide.

If I had known the tears you’d shed,
I swear I wouldn’t have bled
All over the kitchen floor,
My sweet, eternal, Lenore.
